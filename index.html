Go Together

The pandemic over, and it’s time to see the world! With the Go Together app, friends and families can create memorable travel experiences that will reflect the interests of everyone in the group.

MY ROLE: UX designer (individual project with partner contribution)

TIMELINE:  4 weeks

THE PROBLEM: Many people travel to create memorable moments.  Disagreements within groups about what to do creates stress, especially for the main planner.

THE SOLUTION: This app allows people traveling together to create interest profiles, and provides recommendations for activities and places to stay based on common interests.

Research

Interviews

5 interviews were conducted on the topics of post-pandemic travel habits, planning, and desired experiences.

Affinity Diagram

Grouping of responses showed that most people travel in groups, creating both goals and pain points for the planner in regards to keeping the group happy and coordinated.

Empathy Map

An empathy map was created from the perspective of the planner of a trip with multiple companions.

Persona

Jane Snow is a busy, working mother desiring to create a memorable and harmonious family vacation.

Problem Statement

Working parents with demanding jobs struggle to create memorable moments with their families.

How might we design a travel app that will allow parents who plan travel to meet the interests of everyone in the group so that families are able to enjoy stress-free time together?

Solution Process

Brainstorming

I like, I wish, What if lists generated possible features of an app to address the problem

Feature Priority Matrix

Possible features were narrowed down using a feature priority matrix. From the low complexity, high impact quadrant features providing suggestions to meet everyone’s needs were chosen.

1. Jane feels disconnected.

Jane has been working long hours trying to keep up with orders for her compounded nutritional supplements. She feels disconnected from her family.

2. She wants to make her family happy.

She decided to plan a memorable trip. One of her sons wants to go to the beach and just hang out. Her other son wants to go to an amusement park and ride every ride. Her husband just wants great food.

3. She goes to the app.

Jane decided to try the Go Together app for help in planning something to make everyone happy. She enters everyone’s “Likes” and searches for accommodations that will be close proximity to everything they want to do.

4. The app finds 2 great hotels.

The app suggests 2 beach-front hotels within 4 blocks of a “must try” restaurant and within a 15-minute drive of 2 amusement parks.

5. She books hotel with best amenities.

Jane chooses the hotel with discount tickets to an amusement part, a free shuttle service, and a full breakfast buffet.

6. Jane happily starts packing.

Jane starts packing for an amazing trip.

Initial User Flow

Initial user flow with account creation, traveler profile, companion profiles, and trip creation with recommendations based on profiles of all travelers in the group.

Insights and Iterations

Insight: It would be cumbersome for the planner to add all the traveler profiles. 

Iteration: Allow travelers to create and share profiles

Insight: Meaning of profile markers needs clarification.

Iteration: Added text clues

Iteration: Added the user as the first of the companions

Iterated User Flow

Iterated user flow with ability to import shared user profiles rather than having the planner create each profile.
